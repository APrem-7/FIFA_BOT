Introduction
The Netherlands pressed selectively and attacked with structured wide rotations. The USA controlled early midfield phases but lacked penalty-area presence. The Dutch punished defensive spacing with precision.

Performance Indicators
Possession: USA had long spells but the Dutch controlled where the match was played. 
Chance Creation: Netherlands generated higher-quality chances via cut-backs.
Shape Changes: Netherlands used 3-4-1-2 into 5-2-3 defensively; USA used 4-3-3.

Match Analysis
In possession, the Dutch targeted USA full-backs with triangles. Memphis dropped into midfield to connect. USA progressed through Adams and Musah but struggled with final-third decision-making.

Defensive Organization
Netherlands forced play wide, limiting central entries. USA’s high line was exposed on diagonal runs.

Game-State Management
Dutch scored at critical times: early and just before halftime. USA comeback hopes were short-lived as Dumfries finished a late decisive attack.

Discussion
Possession without penetration cost the USA. Netherlands executed in key zones.

Conclusion
Dutch advanced through tactical clarity and clinical finishing.
