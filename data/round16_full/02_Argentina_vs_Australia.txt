Argentina 2–1 Australia | Round of 16 | 2022.12.03

⚽ Messi 35'
⚽ Álvarez 57'
⚽ Goodwin 77'

Introduction
Argentina controlled tempo through compact spacing and staggered midfield roles. Australia defended bravely and countered well late, but Argentina’s control and creativity prevailed.

Performance Indicators
Possession: Argentina controlled midfield.
Chance Creation: Argentina far more dangerous centrally.
Shape: Argentina 4-3-3 into 4-4-2; Australia 4-5-1 block.

Match Analysis
Messi orchestrated from half-spaces. Argentina built via Fernández and De Paul, pushing full-backs high. Australia tried transitions, targeting wide spaces behind Tagliafico.

Defensive Organization
Argentina compact; Australia disciplined but eventually overstretched.

Game-State Management
Argentina scored in each half, then absorbed late pressure.

Discussion
Game showed difference in technical ceiling and space management.

Conclusion
Argentina progressed with controlled performance.
